<threat_model>
| ID | Threat | Severity | Mitigation |
|----|--------|----------|------------|
| T-1002-01 | Browser camera capture is assumed to work everywhere. | medium | Use `input type=file accept=image/* capture=environment` as progressive enhancement with gallery fallback. |
| T-1002-02 | A photo is saved with wrong or missing coordinates. | high | Require confirmation map with draggable marker and reject saves without valid coordinates. |
| T-1002-03 | Geolocation denial blocks the whole feature. | medium | Provide manual map/search fallback and clear accuracy/source state. |
| T-1002-04 | Quick capture creates public photos accidentally. | high | Private create endpoint always creates private images; public action is separate in Plan 3. |
| T-1002-05 | Upload flow duplicates S3 logic and drifts from existing validation. | medium | Reuse/extract existing resize/hash/sign/upload/metadata patterns. |
</threat_model>
